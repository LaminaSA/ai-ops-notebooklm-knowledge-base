## üí° 2. Solution

My manager noticed my interest in AI and automation and informed me about a meeting centred around the use of AI-powered tools in our workplace. I was introduced to NotebookLM and immediately recognised its potential. This wasn‚Äôt just another tool ‚Äî it was an opportunity to lift the weight of our product knowledge burden.

## üìà 4. Impact & Next Steps

I delivered a presentation on my notebooks to the EMEA Deal Hub team. The most satisfying comment was: 
"Are you trying to put us out of a job?"
I don't expect to be made redundant by the notebooks;  I now access consistent product information far faster, and the number of questions on product knowledge has dropped substantially.

NotebookLM has transformed the way I work. I have given my team a centralised, conversational interface instead of navigating 100+ hyperlinks. 

The main limitation is that NotebookLM relies on static PDFs. As products evolve, the knowledge risks becoming outdated. The logical next step is to automate ingestion so the notebooks refresh themselves with new releases and announcements, ensuring accuracy without the version-control nightmare.
